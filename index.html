Hamari Love Story ❤️

Sab kuch ek chhoti si mulaqat se shuru hua. Tum Shilpi didi ke ghar aayi thi, aur us din hum dono ne pehli baar call par ghanton baat ki. Pehli hi baat me tum mera mazaak uda rahi thi, aur main bhi tumhe chhed raha tha. Pata hi nahi chala ki woh hasi-mazaak kab roz ki baaton me badal gaya.

Phir ek din maine tumhari photo ke bahane tumhara number maanga. Lekin sabse pyari baat ye thi ki tumne khud Shilpi didi ke phone se mera number yaad karke mujhe message kiya. Us din se hamari baatein aur bhi badh gayin. Har din... Har raat... Bas ek dusre se baat karna hi sabse khaas pal ban gaya.

Dheere-dheere pata hi nahi chala ki kab hum dono ko ek dusre se itna pyaar ho gaya. Phir tum Surat chali gayi. Jab tum wapas aayi, to shayad ek galatfahmi ki wajah se hum dono ek dusre se door ho gaye. Woh waqt bahut mushkil tha.

Lekin shayad hamari mohabbat itni kamzor nahi thi. Pura ek saal baad, jab Shilpi didi mere ghar aayi, maine unke phone se tumhe phir se message kiya. Tab hume pata chala ki hum dono ke beech sirf ek galatfahmi thi.

Us din ke baad hum phir se ek ho gaye. Aur aaj hume saath me ek saal ho chuka hai. Har din tumhare saath meri zindagi aur bhi khoobsurat lagti hai.

Main tumse ek wada karta hoon...

Chahe zindagi me kitni bhi mushkilein aaye, Main hamesha tumhara saath dunga. Main hamesha tumhare saath rahunga. Tum meri sabse pyari khushi ho, Aur hamesha rahogi. ❤️
